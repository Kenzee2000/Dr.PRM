Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information

Dr Pradeep R Madhivathanan is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your personal data in compliance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. We collect personal information including your name, contact details, medical history and insurance information solely for the purpose of providing anaesthetic and medical care, scheduling appointments, processing insurance claims and communicating with your healthcare team. Your data is never sold or shared for marketing purposes.

We may share your information with hospitals where we practise, your insurance provider for billing and authorisation, other healthcare professionals involved in your care, and regulatory bodies such as the GMC where required by law. We retain medical records in accordance with NHS records management guidelines. Appropriate technical and organisational measures are in place to protect your data from unauthorised access or disclosure.

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your personal data. You also have the right to data portability and to object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact our practice using the details on our contact page. We may update this policy from time to time and any changes will be posted on this page.

If you have any questions or concerns about how your personal data is handled, please contact us at +44 7983 344883 or write to us at London Bridge Hospital, 27 Tooley Street, London, SE1 2PR. This policy was last updated in May 2026.
