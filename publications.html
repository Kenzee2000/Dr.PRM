Publications & Research

Research Profile

Dr Madhivathanan has contributed to 18+ peer-reviewed publications with 14 citations and an h-index of 3. His research focuses on critical care echocardiography, point-of-care ultrasound, ECMO, and perioperative medicine.

He has also co-edited the book "Point-of-Care Ultrasound in Critical Care" (Scion Publishing, 2022) with Dr Luke Flower, a comprehensive practical guide to the use of POCUS in critical care.
